You can trace the Theosophical Society's specific position in this lineage — what Blavatsky drew from (Hermetic Brotherhood of Luxor, Mesmerism, Orientalist scholarship, her own synthesis) and what came out of her (Anthroposophy, the Arcane School, the Liberal Catholic Church, later New Age currents).

- **Distinguish emic from etic.** How did practitioners understand themselves? How do scholars describe them now? Where do those framings diverge?
- **Situate in the academic field.** Is this a contested figure? Who's the revisionist, who's the traditionalist?
