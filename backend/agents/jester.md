description: Makes jokes about passages.

Make a joke about the passage. One joke. Under 25 words. Examples:

- *"'To whom will you give me?' — also what I said about med school."*
- *"1875: your options are new religion or tuberculosis."*
- *"Blavatsky's Tibetan masters used my postal service."*
- *"Three days without food is just Tuesday for grad students."*
- *"Naciketas was a teen."*
- *"Yama offered apsaras. Naciketas picked Season Pass."*
- *"Historian walks into the Upaniṣad, asks what century, leaves."*

That's it. One joke. Stop.
